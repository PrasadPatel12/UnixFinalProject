Joystix's Top 10 Must-Play Playstation Games

10. Uncharted 4: A Thief's End

Uncharted 4 is the sixth release of the Uncharted series. The game continues Nathan Drake's journey who now has to save his brother samuel from a new enemy. Filled with emotions and adventures, this game is easily one of the best franchises of all time.

9. The Last of Us Part II

The Last of Us Part 2 is a continuation of the The Last of Us which occurs 25 years after the brain infection outbreak. The story allows players to play as Ellie, who wants to get revenge on those who are responsible. Explore the world by forming new friendships who will help you along the way. The Last of Us Part 2 has stealth mechanics and a similar overall gameplay compared to the first game released back in 2013.

8. Horizon Zero Dawn

Horizon Zero Dawn tells the story of Aloy, a curious huntress who is treated as an outsider from members of the Nora tribe. In this open world game, players will find clues and uncover secrets regarding Aloy's past and identity. The game features fun fighting mechanisms including the ability to override machines to your advantage. Zero Dawn allows users to go all out or choose a stealthier approach to take down fierce ennemies. Which one will you choose?

7. Fortnite

Another game which recently grew in popularity is Fortnite. Now counting more than 350 million players, this battle royale game has collaborated with multiple artists and organizations. The game is known for it's competitiveness and for offering gamers that sweet sense of achievement whenever they win a victory royale. Play in squads, duos or solo for countless hours of fun!

6. Overwatch

Overwatch is a multiplayer game where teams participate in fun battles to win against other teams. The game consists of many playable characters who each have their own unique skill. Moreover, this game requires strategy since each teammate will have a role whether it's supporting your allies by playing on the defensive side or fighting in close quarters where things can heat up pretty quickly.

5. Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales

It's a bird! It's a plane! It's a web? You guessed right, it's your friendly neighborhood Spider-Man! Along with countless films, comics and video games, Marvel has teamed up with Sony to bring another addition to the Spider-Man series. This time the game will revolve around Miles Morales where players will be able to complete new missions similar to the ones from the previous game. The game will contain familiar fighting mechanics and gameplay where you can spend your time swinging across the city!

4. Grand Theft Auto V

While Grand Theft Auto V can easily be labelled as violent and downright unrealistic, it does a good job of recreating real environments in a fictional game. Indeed, with a big map that users can explore, a friendly and accurate driving mechanism and many customization options, GTA V has proven that it is worthy of ranking amongst the biggest names in the gaming industry.

3. Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

Finding its way to the top three, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is a must-have for any player. Walking down the same path as its predecessors, Rainbow Six Siege is a strategic game involving two teams consisting of operators who are given conflicting missions to win rounds. each round has a limited time so communication with teammates is key. The game is known for constantly receiving new operators with unique abilities from which players can choose.

2. Minecraft

Coming in at the second spot, Minecraft is undoubtedly one of the most popular games ever created. The game created by Mojang Studios has created a virtual environement where gamers can unleash their creativity in a 3D world without any barriers. Survive through the night by facing against skeletons, zombies and spiders or create a masterpiece in creative mode with unlimited items at your fingertips.

1. God of War (2018)

Taking the 1st place of this top 10 list of best games of all time is God of War. While many might argue that God of War should not rank first, the franchise has received a lot of praise along the years. God of War now showcases the life of Kratos from a different perspective. You heard that right, compared to the series' previous releases, the new game takes a new approach by exploring the Norse mythology rather than Greek mythology.
